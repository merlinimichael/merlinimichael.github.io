Humminbird Surveyed and Non-Surveyed Lakes

This project developed a web application for Johnson Outdoors Customer Service Representatives. This application developed a way to access and view the surveyed and non-survyed lakes for Humminbird. It allows customer service representatives to answer customer calls and questions about what lakes are available and on what version of the chip the lake first appeared. Additional information of the lakes is also available through a pop-up dialog box. The additional information does include what county the lake is located in, surface acres, and breif a breif description of the data.
