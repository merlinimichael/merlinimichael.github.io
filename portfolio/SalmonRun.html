The Salmon Run: 2100 mile bike race from Big Sky Country to the Pacific Ocean

This map was a project in creating a mountain bike race route. The race was to have 21 segments. Each segment was 1 day and the distance of approximately 100 miles. This projected incorporated many different aspects from routing to land cover dataset manipulation.
