Shaded Relief and Land Cover for Northwest United States

This map was part of a larger project. This map represents the land cover dataset and the hill shading of the upper northwest region of the United States. The color scheme was modified to allow for a more realistic representation of the land covers. Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator were used to compose this shaded relief map.
